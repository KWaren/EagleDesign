Our Programs

Explore our diverse language programs, designed for all levels to help you achieve fluency and cultural understanding.

General English Courses

The General English Programme at Eagle Vision Institute prepares you for effective and appropriate communication in real-life situations. It is structured in a way that helps you improve your level gradually by acquiring new knowledge and abilities from a beginner’s level to a proficient level across all the key language skills (listening, speaking, reading and writing) with a lot of additional work on grammar, vocabulary and pronunciation. See more....

Professional or Executive English Programs

The Executive English Programme is a bouquet of individually tailored English courses carefully designed to meet your specific communication needs within your current work environment and in an international setting. There are three content areas: General English, Business English and Professional English. After a careful assessment of your work and career development aspirations, we will craft a course content that best meets your specific needs. See more....

Vacation English Programs

The Vacation English Immersion Programme is an exceptional opportunity for students to improve their English in an English-speaking environment while having fun. It is an exciting language learning experience for young learners and adults who combine effective English classes with recreational activities designed to enhance their use of the English language. You can enrol for any period from a month to 3 months between July and September each year. See more....

Computer Studies

Do you want to be a Microsoft Office expert? This program is the best solution. Become an expert within four weeks. The Computer Department of Eagle Vision Institute is a modern and well-resourced division of the Institute set up to equip learners with the computing skills relevant to a successful career in today’s technology-oriented world. TOEFL / IELTS / TOEIC Preparation

Entrepreneurship Training

Every business owner needs core entrepreneurial skills to create and manage their business successfully. Entrepreneurship requires hard work, sacrifice, business knowledge, and complex decision-making. Our Entrepreneurship Training Programme coaches participants in fundamental, practical skills to grow from a start-up to a thriving business. See more....
